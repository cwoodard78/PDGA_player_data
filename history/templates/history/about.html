About Rating Rivals

Rating Rivals is a tool inspired by the passion and competitiveness of disc golfers. We understand that many disc golfers spend a significant amount of time on the PDGA website, checking their peers' ratings to see how they stack up. This project aims to streamline that process and provide easy-to-comprehend, side-by-side comparisons of player ratings.

The mission is to shorten the time it takes to evaluate your standings and to enhance the disc golfing experience by offering a tool that not only saves time but also offers valuable insights into your performance and that of your peers. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive athlete, Rating Rivals is designed to help you keep track of your progress and stay ahead of the game.

Rating Rivals started as a personal tool, but after realizing how many others would be interested in using it, I decided to turn it into a web app. I have spent my personal time and money to bring this to the community. If you enjoy using Rating Rivals, please consider leaving me a tip by buying a coffee. I'm also interested in hearing your feedback.
