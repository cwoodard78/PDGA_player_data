Rating Rivals is a tool designed to help disc golfers compare their ratings with peers. It provides side-by-side comparisons of player ratings over time.

The data is responsibly retrieved from the PDGA website, specifically the player history pages. This ensures up-to-date and accurate comparisons based on the PDGA numbers provided in the search bar.
